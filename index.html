About the hotel
A beloved family-run retreat in the southwest of Bansko
Location
76 Stefan Karadja St., Bansko 2770 — quiet southwest corner with panoramic Pirin mountain views. Next to the gondola ski lift, 10 min walk to the historic centre.
Hosts
Krasimir & Maria — celebrated by guests across all platforms for going far beyond typical hospitality. Homemade wine, personal ski transfers, guided hikes and more.
Rooms
Double, twin, triple rooms and 2 mansard apartments for up to 4 persons. All with private bath, WC, cable TV, Wi-Fi, mini-bar, balcony and mountain views.
Dining
Generous buffet breakfast daily. Restaurant-tavern with traditional Bulgarian cuisine, homemade bread & pastries, home-brewed wine. Dinner available on request.
